About

The Le Blanc Resto was founded in the 1890s by a French chef named Ms. Emilia Evaine Le Blanc.
It is because of her passion and dedication to cooking which led us to where we are now. The Le Blanc
Resto will continue to serve its guests with the same taste for generations to come.
